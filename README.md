Clicker-Game

General Idea:
A game similar to custom maps where you can practice your mouse clicking accuracy. 

General Structure:
A section that holds targets to be clicked on. 
    Goals:
        targets appearing sequentially 1 at a time
        targets appearing in groups, groups appreaing sequentially
        Difficulty: incorporating different sizes for different difficulties.
        Variation: random shapes and colours
        Make the grid auto fit!!!!!

A section with game controls and score.
    Start, pause, clear, score.
    Goals:
        ammount of targets input. 
        dropdown for style.
        color control.

Design decisions:
generate all targets at beginning and manipulate visibility based on actions, or generate random
targets as they get clicked on
